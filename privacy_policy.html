Privacy Policy for Uplift

Last updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Uplift ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI therapy companion mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Audio Recordings

We collect audio recordings when you use voice features for therapy sessions. Audio is:

  • Processed to generate text transcriptions
  • Used to provide AI-powered therapeutic responses
  • Encrypted during transmission
  • Deleted after session processing unless you choose to save session history

2.2 Session Data

We collect:

  • Session transcripts and summaries
  • Mood tracking data you provide
  • Session timestamps and duration
  • Action items and insights generated

2.3 Account Information

We collect account information through Firebase Authentication:

  • Email address (if using email authentication)
  • Phone number (if using phone authentication)
  • Google account information (if using Google Sign-In)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered therapy sessions
  • Generate personalized insights and recommendations
  • Track your emotional progress over time
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Maintain app security

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Stored securely using Firebase and Google Cloud Platform
  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Stored on secure servers in the United States
  • Protected by industry-standard security measures

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Google Cloud Platform, Firebase, OpenAI API (for AI processing)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your session history
  • Opt-out of mood tracking sync

7. Data Retention

  • Session data: Retained until you delete it or your account
  • Mood entries: 60-day rolling retention
  • Account data: Retained until account deletion

8. Children's Privacy

Uplift is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Authentication, database, analytics
  • OpenAI API: AI conversation processing
  • Google Cloud: Storage and processing

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Important Disclaimer

Uplift is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: 911
